01Getting in

A trainer will create your account and send you an email + temporary password. The app lives at cbdcollege-scheduler.pages.dev.

First sign-in

1Open the app URL in your phone's browser (or laptop).
2Enter the email and temporary password your trainer gave you.
3Tap Sign In.
Change your password Ask your trainer to reset it to something only you know — temporary passwords should not stay in use.

02Install the app on your phone

Installing the app puts a CBD Scheduler icon on your home screen and unlocks mobile push notifications. Strongly recommended.

Android (Chrome)

1Sign in and look at the header — you'll see a 📱 Install button.
2Tap it.
3Tap Install when Chrome prompts.
4The app icon appears on your home screen. Open the app from there from now on.

iPhone (Safari)

1Sign in. Tap the 📱 Install button.
2An instructions modal appears. Tap the Share icon at the bottom of Safari (square with an up-arrow).
3Scroll the share sheet and tap Add to Home Screen.
4Tap Add.
5Open the CBD Scheduler icon from your home screen — not from Safari — to get the full PWA experience.

03Turn on mobile notifications

So your phone buzzes when your roster changes or a trainer sends you a message.

1Open the app (on iPhone, from the home-screen icon — not Safari).
2Tap 🔔 Enable Push in the header.
3Your phone shows a system prompt — tap Allow.
4You'll see a toast saying "Mobile push enabled" and the Enable Push button disappears.
You're set From now on, your phone receives a real OS notification whenever your roster changes, even if the app is closed.

04The header — every button explained

  • Your name + ASSISTANT badge — confirms you're signed in.
  • 📱 Install — install the PWA (mobile only; hides once installed).
  • 🔔 — notification bell. Red badge = unread count. Tap to read.
  • 🔔 Enable Push — opt into mobile push notifications. Hides once you've granted permission.
  • 🌙 / ☀️ — dark / light theme toggle.
  • Sign Out — end your session.

The two tabs

My Roster My Availability
  • My Roster — your personal schedule. Read-only.
  • My Availability — where you tell trainers when you can and can't work.

05Reading the My Roster calendar

A month view of every day, with a clear icon showing whether you're rostered or not.

What the icons mean

✓ You're rostered that day (green tint) ✕ A class runs but you're not on (red tint) No class scheduled (neutral) Today (blue ring + TODAY label)

Navigation

1Use ‹ and › above the calendar to change month.
2Tap Today to jump back to the current month.
3Tap any day cell to open its full detail.

06Checking when your next shift is

Above the calendar is a blue Next Shift hero card with at-a-glance info.

What it shows

  • Today — date, today's class times, total students, and whether you're working today (YOU'RE WORKING badge) or not (NOT NEEDED).
  • Next shift — when your next rostered day is (Tomorrow, In 3 days, etc.) with date, class times, your own start time, and the role (Head Trainer / Assistant).
  • Working with — the names of the other staff rostered that day, with the Head Trainer flagged.
If nothing's coming up The card shows "You're not currently rostered for any upcoming days". Check your availability is up to date — see Section 8.

07What the day-detail modal shows

Tap any day on the calendar to open the day-detail view.

Inside you'll see:

  • The day name + full date.
  • Class times (AM and PM).
  • Student counts (AM / PM).
  • Whether the session is at capacity (CAP badge).
  • The list of staff rostered, sorted with the Head Trainer at the top, each tagged with their role.
  • Your own start time if you're rostered.

08The My Availability tab

Where you tell trainers when you can and can't work. Auto-rostering relies on this — keep it current.

Reading the cells

Available Not available Not set (counts as available by default)

09Setting your availability

One day at a time

1Tap the day on the calendar.
2Choose Available or Not Available.
3Optional: add a note (e.g. "doctor's appointment").
4Tap Save.
5To remove a set value, open the same day and tap Clear.

Recurring patterns

If you have a recurring schedule (e.g. "available every Monday"), tap the Patterns button at the top of the tab. The pattern editor lets you toggle each day-of-week as a default — the calendar then applies that pattern to every future date.

Individual day overrides always win over the pattern. So if your default is available Mondays but you set not available on one specific Monday, that Monday stays as not-available even though the pattern says otherwise.

When to update

  • Before the trainer auto-rosters the next month — so the system uses correct data.
  • As soon as you know about a date you can't work — even months in advance.
  • You don't need to mark every available day — leaving a day blank means "available unless told otherwise".

10Receiving notifications

Two places

  1. The bell icon in the header. A red badge shows how many unread notifications you have. Tap to open the list.
  2. Your phone's notification centre — a real OS notification (lock screen / banner) for each event, as long as you tapped 🔔 Enable Push earlier.

What kinds of notifications you'll see

  • "Your roster was updated" — when a trainer changes your shift for any date.
  • "Your availability was updated" — when a trainer overrides one of your available/unavailable days.
  • "Default class times updated" — when class start/end times shift across multiple days of the week.
  • "Assistant start times updated" — when the slot start-time table changes.
  • Manual announcements — anything a trainer sends from the Notifications tab.

Marking a notification read

Tap any item in the panel — the unread dot disappears and the bell badge counts down.

11Dark mode & preferences

Switching theme

Tap the 🌙 (moon) icon in the header to switch to light mode; tap ☀️ (sun) to go back to dark. Your choice is saved on this device.

Default

New visitors land in dark mode. If you'd rather have light, just toggle once and it'll remember.

12Signing out & switching devices

Sign out

Tap Sign Out in the header.

Using multiple devices

You can sign in on a phone, a tablet, and a desktop at the same time. Each device manages its own push subscription, so notifications buzz on whichever device you've enabled push for.

If you change phones

Install the app on the new phone, sign in, and tap 🔔 Enable Push. The old device keeps its subscription until you sign out — sign out on the old phone so notifications stop going there.

13Troubleshooting

I tap a day on My Roster but nothing happens

The My Roster tab is read-only. Tapping a day opens a detail view but you can't edit. To change when you're available, use the My Availability tab instead.

The Enable Push button doesn't appear

You've probably already enabled it on this device, or your browser blocks notifications. Check your browser's site settings to confirm.

My phone isn't getting pushes

  • iPhone — confirm you installed the PWA via the Install button, and that you opened the app from the home-screen icon (not Safari) when tapping Enable Push.
  • Android — confirm Chrome notifications aren't muted at the OS level.
  • Try signing out and back in; then tap Enable Push again.

My next shift hero card is wrong

It reflects what the trainer has saved in the database. If you think it's wrong, contact your trainer — they may have rolled out an auto-roster that hasn't yet refreshed in your cache. Pull-to-refresh on mobile or hit reload on desktop.

I forgot my password

Ask your trainer to reset it from the Admin tab.

I see someone else's roster

That shouldn't be possible — if you do, sign out immediately and tell your trainer. Your account may be linked to the wrong staff record.
